It takes some warming up to but give it a chance

Reviewed on 16 December 2013

" I purchased this product based solely on the description and reviews alone. This is my first Jo Malone experience. My friend wears Red Roses by Jo Malone, and I love that scent on her. So when purchasing, I just assumed this would be a richer and softer version of Red Roses. When it arrived, I was so excited, that I tried it on immediately and immediately hated it.

To me it smelled like a cheap French perfume of the 80's. The kind you find at the dollar store. I was just about to package it back up for return, but based on the positive reviews I read, I decided to wait and try the scent a couple more times. I have now grown to love this scent and can't stop smelling my wrists on a minute-by-minute basis. The longer it sits on your skin, the richer, and more settled the scent becomes. Chocolate and velvet oud gives off a soft musky scent while, while the rose comes through subtly and with a little bit of spice bite at the end. "
