Details

The world of Daisy offers a fresh feminine youth. This floral fragrance offers a filled notes of wild strawberry, violet petals and jasmine. Sophisticated yet playful, this scent compliments the bottle design of a luxurious block of clear glass topped with daisies blooming out of the rounded gold cap.

Notes: Strawberry, Violet Leaves, Ruby Red Grapefruit, Gardenia, Violet Petals, Jasmine Petals, Musk, Vanilla, White Woods.
